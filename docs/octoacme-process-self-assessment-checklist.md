# OctoAcme Project Management Self-Assessment Checklist

Use this checklist quarterly or after major process changes to identify strengths and improvement areas.

- [ ] Are all project roles documented and understood?
- [ ] Is every project initiated with a clear business case and stakeholder alignment?
- [ ] Are Definition of Done and acceptance criteria required for ALL backlog items?
- [ ] Are risks reviewed at least weekly and assigned clear owners?
- [ ] Are requirements and designs reviewed by both technical and product leads?
- [ ] Are retrospectives generating tracked action items, and is their progress reviewed?
- [ ] Are communication and escalation paths known to all team members?
- [ ] Do onboarding materials for each role exist and are they up to date?
- [ ] Are PRs small, reviewed, and tested before merge?
- [ ] Is end-to-end user impact tracked after every release?
